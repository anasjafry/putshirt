About Us

We are a team of students from IIT Madras who are passionate about tee shirts. 'We wear what we would love to!'. Through our endeavour, "Put T-Shirt", the name being inspired by our institutes's lingo, our vision is to bring about a culture of tee shirt enthusiasts, who wish to make a statement about their clothing choices and world views.

On engineering terms, our motto is to Design, Develop and Deliver! From the collection of designs on the website, you can place your orders. All tee shirts which receive more than 100 orders will be released. Make the advance payment and get your favourite tees delivered at your doorsteps! It takes not more than 10 days!
